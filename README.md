**Goal:** land recurring **fleet detailing** accounts within a **15-mile radius of downtown Dallas**, run as a **mobile** operation (we come to them).

This repo is Rob's working kit. It has two things right now:

1. **A researched lead list** — ~70 real, named businesses in the radius that run vehicle fleets, organized by segment and ranked into a priority hit list.
2. **An outreach kit** — how to approach fleet accounts, what to say (call / text / email / walk-up scripts), how to price recurring contracts, and the buying signals to keep hunting for on your own.

> **Phase 2 (coming next):** a full go-to-market (GTM) plan that builds on this. The repo is structured so it drops in cleanly.

## ⚠️ Read this before you drive anywhere

These leads were assembled from public web sources. **Treat them as a starting point, not gospel:**
- **Verify the address and phone** on the source link before you show up — small lots move and close.
- **Confidence flags** (High / Medium / Low) on each lead tell you how well-confirmed it is.
- **Fleet sizes** marked "signal" are inferred (hiring posts, "our trucks" photos) — confirm the real count on the first call.
- A few leads sit right at the 15-mile edge; they're flagged. Decide your own hard cutoff.

The point of the list isn't to be perfect — it's to put 60+ real doors in front of you so you're never wondering "who do I call next."
