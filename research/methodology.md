# Methodology & Data Caveats

How this lead list was built, so Rob (and future-us, in Phase 2) knows exactly what he's looking at.

## What we did

- Defined the target as **recurring fleet detailing** for a **mobile** operator within **~15 miles of downtown Dallas** (32.7767, -96.7970).
- Split the market into 5 segments and ran a dedicated web-research pass on each:
  1. Auto dealers, auctions & recon lots
  2. Branded service-company fleets (HVAC / plumbing / electrical / pest)
  3. Rentals, limo / black-car & medical transport
  4. Hotels, country clubs, property mgmt & senior living
  5. Landscaping, food trucks, courier, towing, RV/powersports
- Each lead was sourced from a **real, citable web page** (company site, listing, or directory). Source links are in every leads file.

## Geography rule

**In-zone:** Dallas (incl. Stemmons/Harry Hines, Oak Cliff, Pleasant Grove, Love Field), Irving / Las Colinas, Grand Prairie, Mesquite, Garland, Richardson, Farmers Branch, Carrollton, Addison, Cedar Hill, DeSoto, Duncanville, Balch Springs.

**Excluded as too far:** Plano, Frisco, McKinney, Allen, Lewisville, Flower Mound, Mansfield, Fort Worth. Several otherwise-good businesses were dropped for being just outside the ring — those exclusions are noted at the bottom of each leads file so we don't "rediscover" them later.

**Edge cases** (~15 mi, like DoubleTree DFW North) are flagged in-line. Rob should set his own hard cutoff.

## Confidence flags — what they mean

- **High** — business existence, in-zone location, and fleet signal all confirmed from a solid source.
- **Medium** — business and fleet are real, but exact address or current fleet count wasn't fully cross-confirmed.
- **Low** — real business, but the fleet signal or details are thin; verify before investing time.

## Known limitations (read these)

- **No live phone verification.** Addresses/phones come from web pages that may be stale. **Call/verify before driving.**
- **Fleet sizes are often inferred** from hiring posts or "our trucks" photos, not official counts. Confirm on the first call.
- **Some sites blocked automated checks** (a few limo/black-car sites returned errors). They're real and Dallas-serving, but their exact base needs a human confirm.
- **Decision-maker names aren't included** — only the *role* to ask for. Names change too fast to be worth listing; get them on the call.
- **This is a snapshot**, not a live feed. Re-run a segment search any time the list thins out (see [finding leads](../outreach/03-signals-and-finding-leads.md)).

## Gaps worth a follow-up pass

- **In-zone solar installers** with a branded truck fleet (none cleanly found — most DFW solar serves the northern suburbs).
- **In-zone boat dealers** (MarineMax is in Lewisville, out of zone) — try a Lake Ray Hubbard / Garland-side search.
- **SMU & University of Dallas** campus fleets (almost certainly exist; specifics weren't sourced).
- **Kahn Mechanical** (commercial HVAC, "fleet of fully-stocked service vehicles") — verify yard is in-zone.
