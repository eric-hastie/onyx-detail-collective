# Auto Dealers, Auctions & Recon Lots

**Why this segment:** the classic, highest-volume fleet-detailing customer. Dealers and auctions recondition every used unit before it sells — that's constant, repeatable work, and a mobile detailer can service it right on the lot.

## 🎯 Clusters & corridors — canvass these in one trip

1. **Harry Hines Blvd (75229/75220), NW Dallas** — densest independent/wholesale strip in the zone. 8–10 lots in a 2-mile stretch. **Start here.**
2. **Forest Lane dealer row, Garland (75042)** — CityWide, The Car Network, Cars & Credit Master within ~1.5 mi.
3. **E. R.L. Thornton Fwy franchise row, E. Dallas (75228)** — Clay Cooley Chevrolet, Cowboy Toyota, Norm Reeves side by side. Ask for recon managers.
4. **E. Jefferson Blvd, Oak Cliff (75203)** — dense small-lot BHPH cluster; owner-operators who buy per car.
5. **E. Main St, Grand Prairie (75050)** — Star Auto Group + Graff Chevrolet anchor a stretch.

**Excluded (out of radius):** AutoPark Dallas, Nissan/Mitsubishi of Lewisville (Lewisville); America's Auto Auction HQ, Manheim DFW (both Plano).
