# Rentals, Limo / Black-Car & Medical Transport

**Why this segment:** for these businesses **cleanliness *is* the product.** An exotic rental, a black-car ride, or a patient-transport van that isn't spotless loses the customer. Many can't take vehicles to a shop (open 24/7, big vehicles, tight turnaround) — which makes **mobile the only practical option.**

> ⚠️ Several limo sites blocked automated checks — they're real and Dallas-serving, but confirm their physical base is inside the 15-mile ring when you call. Confirm fleet counts on the first call.
