# Landscaping, Food Trucks, Courier, Towing & RV/Powersports

**Why this segment:** a catch-all of high-potential niches a mobile detailer can service on-site. Some are **single-yard, high-density** (a landscaping yard or food-truck commissary with dozens of trucks in one place); others are **recon-detail** plays (RV/powersports dealers prepping used inventory).

> ⚠️ For chain landscapers, the decision-maker is the **local branch/ops manager**, not corporate — approach the yard directly. Verify in-zone address before driving.
