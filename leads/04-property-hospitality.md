# Hotels, Country Clubs, Property Management & Senior Living

**Why this segment:** organizations that run **shuttle, valet, and maintenance vehicle fleets on-site**, with big parking lots and a brand standard for appearance. Mobile fits perfectly — they'd rather not move vehicles off-property. These are **slower, relationship-based sales** (you're dealing with a facilities or operations manager), but a single account can mean many vehicles, especially the multifamily portfolios.

> ⚠️ Country clubs are strong by category but a *shuttle* fleet couldn't be confirmed at each — the surest pitch there is **valet + grounds/maintenance vehicles.** Confirm fleet specifics on the first call.
