# Branded Service-Company Fleets

**Why this segment:** the **easiest cold sell** for a mobile detailer. These companies run wrapped trucks and vans where appearance *is* marketing — a dirty branded truck in a customer's driveway hurts the brand. Many have **no current detailer**. Sweet spot is ~5–50 trucks: big enough for recurring work, small enough you're not competing with national fleet-wash contractors.
