# ⭐ Priority Hit List — start with these

Out of ~70 leads, these are the highest-value, best-verified targets. They're chosen on three things: **(1) recurring volume, (2) a real pain a mobile detailer solves, and (3) you can actually reach the decision-maker.**

The logic: **one big account or one dense cluster beats ten scattered cold calls.** A single auction contract or one 60-truck plumbing fleet can be worth more than a month of retail jobs.

## Your first week (suggested)

1. **Mon — Call the 4 easy "yes" fleets** (Chapman, Add-All, Romney, plus one more). Goal: book one trial detail. A quick win funds the gas.
2. **Tue — Drive the Harry Hines + Forest Lane clusters.** Walk in, hand a card, quote per-car recon. Owners are on-site.
3. **Wed — Make the 3 big-account calls** (United Caterers, Manheim recon manager, Hot Shot Final Mile). These are longer sales; plant the seed now.
4. **Thu — Hit the exotic-rental trio** (Roma, Platinum, Black Label) — Uptown, all close together.
5. **Fri — Two NEMT calls + follow up** on anyone from Mon–Thu who said "send me info."

Track every call in a simple sheet: *business, who you spoke to, outcome, next step, date.* That tracker becomes the backbone of the Phase-2 GTM plan.
